About

Hi! My name is Kwesi Afrifa, and I'm an 18 year old freshman at MIT studying Computer and Urban Science. I made this website to provide easy, unfiltered access to the nation's bills of parliament and to encourage informed discussion and debate surrounding policy in Ghana. Hope you enjoy!

This is a beta version, so feel free to send feedback, questions or suggestions for new features to my email address.

Connect with me on LinkedIn, Twitter, or Instagram. Alternatively, view my other projects here.
